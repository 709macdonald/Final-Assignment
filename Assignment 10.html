Peter MacDonald

Just trying to be a producer.

Peter MacDonald is a producer, sound engineer, mix engineer, and performer from St.John’s Newfoundland. He is currently in his fourth year at Berklee college of Music obtaining his B.P.S. in the professional studies of Music production. Peter is Avid certified, a union member, and has over 8 years of experience in the music production world. He was the main sound tech at “The Fat Cat” on George Street and has teched across Newfoundland in venues from the LSPU Hall to The Arts and Culture Centre. He has also had the pleasure to preform in every major city across Canada as well as Berlin, Germany.
